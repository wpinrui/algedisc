5. Multiplication Frame — Tab 2

Tab 2 is an HTML table where students fill in products. Two modes are shown: expansion (headers pre-filled, body blank) and factorisation (everything blank). Cells use a term-picker popover — no free-text input.

Expansion: (x + 2)(x + 3)

Factor headers are pre-filled. Student clicks each body cell to place the product term.

6. Dark Mode

Auto-detects OS preference via prefers-color-scheme. A toggle in the header allows manual override. Preference is persisted in localStorage.

Toggle placement

A small sun/moon icon button sits in the header, right-aligned before the tab bar. Minimal footprint — no label, just the icon.

Disc palette — dark mode adjustments

Positive fills are brightened so they don't vanish into the dark workspace. Negative fills are slightly muted (not pure white-pastels) to reduce glare. The hue relationships are preserved — only lightness shifts.
